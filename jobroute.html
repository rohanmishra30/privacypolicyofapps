Privacy Policy

JobRoute — Last updated: May 2026

At JobRoute, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use JobRoute:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (during registration)
  • Date of birth and gender (for profile setup)
  • Educational details — college, course, graduation year
  • Career preferences — target role, skills, city
  • Resume content (when you use our Resume Builder or Analyzer)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay)
  • Device information and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized career guidance and recommendations
  • Power our AI-based career quiz and resume analyzer
  • Connect you with verified HR mentors
  • Process subscription payments and session bookings
  • Send important notifications about your sessions and account
  • Improve our platform and services

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase — an industry-standard platform with enterprise-grade security. We implement the following measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict unauthorized access
  • Payment data is never stored on our servers — handled by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and storage
  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Groq AI — AI career chatbot and resume analysis
  • Calendly — HR mentor session scheduling

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them separately.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only:

  • With HR mentors you choose to book sessions with (name and email only)
  • With payment processors to complete transactions
  • When required by law or legal proceedings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by JobRoute
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@jobroute.in

7. Children's Privacy

JobRoute is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies & Analytics

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of JobRoute after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
