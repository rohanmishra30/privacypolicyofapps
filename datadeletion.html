Delete Your Data

JobRoute — Data Deletion Request

At JobRoute, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

1

Delete from App (Recommended)

Open JobRoute app → Go to Profile → Settings → Scroll to bottom → Tap "Delete Account" → Confirm deletion

2

Request via Email

Send an email to support@jobroute.in with subject "Data Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

3

Verification

We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days.

4

Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

❌ Permanently Deleted:

  • Your name, email, phone number
  • Date of birth and personal details
  • Educational and career information
  • Resume data and uploaded files
  • Career quiz results and history
  • AI chatbot conversation history
  • HR mentor session history
  • Payment history and subscription data
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • All preferences and settings

✅ Data We May Retain:

  • Transaction records required by Indian law (GST/financial records — up to 7 years)
  • Anonymized analytics data (not linked to your identity)
  • Legal compliance records if required by court order

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, resume, career history, and session records will be permanently removed.

Contact Us for Deletion Request

Send deletion request to:

support@jobroute.in

Subject: Data Deletion Request | Include your registered email

We respond within 2 business days

Timeline

  • Request received → Acknowledged within 24 hours
  • Identity verified → Data deletion initiated within 3 business days
  • Complete deletion → Within 30 days of request
  • Confirmation email → Sent after deletion is complete

Questions?
