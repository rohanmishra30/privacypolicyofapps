NoScroll Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to NoScroll. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, stores, and uses information when you use NoScroll.

1. Information We Collect

NoScroll is designed with a privacy-first approach.

  • User preferences and focus settings
  • Habit and productivity data stored locally on your device
  • Focus session statistics and app usage information
  • Reminder and notification preferences

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Local Device Storage

Most data inside NoScroll is stored locally on your device using local storage technologies. This allows the app to work smoothly without requiring account creation or cloud syncing.

3. Notifications

NoScroll may send local notifications for:

  • Focus session reminders
  • Habit tracking reminders
  • Task completion alerts
  • Motivational productivity reminders

4. Permissions

Depending on the features you use, NoScroll may request permissions such as:

  • Notification permission
  • Foreground activity permissions
  • App usage access (for focus tools)

These permissions are only used to improve the focus experience and are never misused.

5. Third-Party Services

NoScroll may use trusted third-party services for analytics, crash monitoring, or app performance improvements in future updates.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and ensure a secure user experience.

7. Children’s Privacy

NoScroll is not intended for children under the age of 13 without parental supervision.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
